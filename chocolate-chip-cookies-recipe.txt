INGREDIENTS:
	
	1C b sugar, packed
	1C butter
	1/2C sugar
	2 eggs
	2tsp vanilla
	2.5C flour
	1tsp baking soda
	1tsp salt
	1C oatmeal
	2C chocolate chips (dark, milk, white, butterscotch, etc.)
	
METHOD:
	Preheat oven to 375F/190C
	Cream sugars and butter.  Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.  Add dry ingedients and mix well.
	Add oatmeal and chocolate chips.
	Use a spoon to place .75" balls onto a cookie sheet.
	Bake 10-12 min.  Makes appox. 4 dozen.
